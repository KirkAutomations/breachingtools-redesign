Technology

Engineered to Dominate

Combat-tested engineering. Aerospace-grade materials. Every spec earned its place.

Anti-Blowback Floating Mass

Internal floating mass absorbs impact energy. Eliminates blowback and vibration. Unique to Double-Tap. Zero energy wasted on the operator.

Aerospace-Grade Materials

Ti-6Al-4V titanium. 6061T6 aluminum. 303 stainless. 4140 chromoly. Machined from solid billets—never cast.

Billet Machined Precision

CNC-machined from solid metal. No casting, no weak points, no air pockets. Uniform grain structure for maximum impact resistance.

Hardcoat Anodized

.002" deep hard anodized aluminum. Second hardest natural substance after diamond. Corrosion-proof. Any environment.

Lightest on the Market

Collapsible design. Positive locking shaft. Maximum breach force at minimum operator weight. Every ounce earns its place.

Made in the USA

Our Story

Born in Combat.
Built in Fayetteville.

BCM Inc. started as a welding company in 2004 in Fayetteville, North Carolina—home of Fort Liberty and the 82nd Airborne. By mid-2006, a master breacher with real-world operational experience shifted the company’s focus to what he knew best: breaching tools that actually work when lives depend on them.

Since January 2007, Double-Tap tools have been in the hands of operators across law enforcement, military, fire departments, and SWAT teams worldwide. The philosophy is simple: build the tool you’d trust your own life to.

Every tool features the patented anti-blowback floating mass system—technology that exists because a breacher understood what engineers couldn’t: the difference between hitting a door in a lab and hitting one at 3 AM with your team stacked behind you.
